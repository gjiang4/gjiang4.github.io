七月詞彙表 📚 July Vocab List

這個月影片教過的字，按最新排序。點 ▶ 聽發音和例句。Tap ▶ to hear the word and examples.

01 persuasive /pərˈsweɪsɪv/
adj.有說服力的per·SUA·sive
persuade (v.) · persuasion (n.)
A great answer doesn't push, it persuades.
Her pitch was so persuasive the whole board agreed.
重音在 SWAY，不是 PER
02 consequential /ˌkɑːnsəˈkwenʃl/
adj.影響重大的con·se·QUEN·tial
consequence (n.) · consequently (adv.)
The most consequential decision of my career.
Small choices can be surprisingly consequential.
比 important 高級
03 provocative /prəˈvɑːkətɪv/
adj.挑釁的／引人深思的pro·VOC·a·tive
provoke (v.) · provocation (n.)
That was a provocative question.
He made a provocative comment just to start a debate.
看語境，挑釁或挑逗
04 penetrate /ˈpenətreɪt/
v.穿透／打進PEN·e·trate
penetration (n.) · penetrating (adj.)
We need to penetrate the overseas market.
It took years to penetrate the Japanese market.
商業常用，別想歪
05 obliterate /əˈblɪtəreɪt/
v.徹底摧毀o·BLIT·er·ate
obliteration (n.)
Germany obliterated them seven to one.
The new model obliterated every sales record.
完全抹平的「毀滅」
06 arson /ˈɑːrsn/
n.縱火AR·son
arsonist (n.)
It wasn't an accident, it was arson.
Police are investigating the fire as arson.
故意放火
07 amateur /ˈæmətər/
n./adj.業餘／外行AM·a·teur
amateurish (adj.)
Don't sound like an amateur in the interview.
He plays guitar, but only as an amateur.
唸 AM-uh-ter
08 assassin /əˈsæsɪn/
n.刺客as·SAS·sin
assassinate (v.) · assassination (n.)
A hired assassin.
The film is about a retired assassin.
重音第二音節
09 strait /streɪt/
n.海峽／困境strait
Ships pass through the Taiwan Strait every day.
After losing his job he was in dire straits.
跟 straight 同音不同拼
10 charming /ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋ/
adj.迷人的CHARM·ing
charm (n./v.) · charmer (n.)
He's charming in interviews.
She has a charming smile that puts everyone at ease.
討喜、有魅力
11 comely /ˈkʌmli/
adj.清秀的（書面）COME·ly
A comely young woman.
The portrait showed a comely figure by the window.
老派但高級
12 askhole /ˈæskhoʊl/
n.愛亂問又不聽的人（俚）ASK·hole
He's a total askhole, always asking and never listening.
Stop being an askhole and just take the advice.
玩笑字，別對老闆說
13 analysis /əˈnæləsɪs/
n.分析a·NAL·y·sis
analyze (v.) · analyst (n.) · analytical (adj.)
Let me walk you through my analysis.
The report includes a detailed cost analysis.
重音第二音節
14 organism /ˈɔːrɡənɪzəm/
n.生物／有機體OR·gan·ism
organic (adj.) · organ (n.)
A living organism.
Even the smallest organism plays a role in the ecosystem.
音節都唸清楚
15 Uranus /ˈjʊrənəs/
n.天王星U·ra·nus
Uranus is the seventh planet from the sun.
Scientists discovered new rings around Uranus.
唸 YOO-rə-nəs，避開另一種唸法
16 slant-eye /ˈslænt aɪ/
n.瞇瞇眼（歧視語）SLANT·eye
This is a racial slur. Recognize it so you can avoid it.
If someone uses it about you, it is offensive, not a compliment.
貶義詞，聽懂、避開、別用
